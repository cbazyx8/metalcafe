About Metal Cafe

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Metal cafe started with a simple mission: to connect people with their favorite restaurants and deliver delicious meals right to their doorstep. What began as a small startup has grown into a trusted food delivery service.

Our Mission

We strive to make food delivery accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone. Our platform connects customers with local restaurants, ensuring quick delivery while maintaining food quality.
